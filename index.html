About Me

I'm Prozan Bogdan-Mădălin, a 2nd Year student at the Faculty of Automation, Computers, Electrical and Electronics Engineering in Galati. I code out of passion — from Python apps and 2D games, to mods published on NexusMods and web projects. I excel at prompt engineering, playtesting, and debugging — finding bugs in application code and games.

I have experience in RE Engine modding (REFramework + Lua) for the game PRAGMATA, with mods published on NexusMods and used by the community. I'm the founder and IGL of a competitive Dead by Daylight eSports team.

Beyond coding: MC and presenter at school events on the stage of the “Fani Tardini” Dramatic Theatre in Galati, Erasmus+ participant in Croatia, volunteer with children with Down syndrome, guitarist and competitive ballroom dancer.
